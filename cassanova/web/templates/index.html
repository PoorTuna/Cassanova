Size-Tiered Compaction (STCS)

Stack 4 identical sstables in one bucket to merge them into the next level. There is no ceiling — L4, L5 and beyond are reachable and score geometrically.

Token Ring

Twelve sectors, eight rows each. An overflow drop ends the run. Arrows steer, Space flushes the current piece into the selected sector.

Tombstones (T)

On an empty bucket a tombstone counts down 10 turns then is reclaimed. On an occupied bucket it becomes a shadow delete: it removes the tile directly beneath it after 3 turns, then removes itself. Four tombstones stacked in a row purge together for a flat bonus.

Hold & Preview

H swaps the current piece with the hold slot, once per turn. The NEXT queue previews the two upcoming pieces.

nodetool

C cleanup — purge every tombstone on the ring.
R repair — remove the top tile of the selected sector.
M minor compact — force-merge the lowest same-level run in the selected sector.
Earned at 1k / 5k / 20k / 50k. Capped at 3 each.

Chain Multiplier

Compactions resolving within one second stack a multiplier up to 8×. Any turn-consuming action resets it.

Read Hot

Every 50 drops one sector flares for 10 seconds. Drops into that sector score double and stack with the chain multiplier.
